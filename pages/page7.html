Un peu d'anglais : Why do you like reptiles ?

Le reste de cette page sera entiérement écrite en anglais

Since I was a kid, I was fascinated by reptiles because I thought
they were related to dinosaurs, but today I know that dinosaurs are more
related to reptiles. Nowadays, this passion is still growing,
and I recently learned a lot about reptiles. In my opinion, reptiles
are a category of animal that are very interesting, due to the diversity of animals
we can put inside this category like: crocodile, snake,
lizard and turtle, inside each of them we have some beautiful has
we used to see with the GreenTree python
The other thing I really like about reptiles is I learned
a lot of scientific things with them,
like what was parthenogenesis or Ontogenetic niche shift.
I also learned a lot about how genetics work with reptiles like snakes and
how someone can bring out some colorful species that don't exist in the wild like those.
In my opinion, reptiles are animals that are underrated inside the domestics animal,
animal like snake and lizard are more docile that what cliché inside fiction spread about them,
reptiles are calm, interesting to watch and interact, they have a long lifespan
(~20 years old, some kind of reptiles like ball python can live more than 20 years)
compared to animals likes dogs, cat or hamster.
The negative points with these types of animals are
that some of them need a big enclosure, a UV lamp and need to be heated,
and some of them especially imported the reptile are easy to stress, but in terms of food,
plus inside the enclosure reptiles don't need so much food compared to dogs or cats,
they also don't have to walk,
